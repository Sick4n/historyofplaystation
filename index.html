Ken Kutaragi

The "Father of the PlayStation" began his professional life in 1975, when he joined the research department of Sony Corporation. During this time, he developed a solid reputation as a brilliant engineer and forward-thinking problem solver. Kutaragi was appointed as Sony Computer Entertainment, Inc.'s president and CEO in 1999.
